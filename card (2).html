Tiger

Tigers are excellent swimmers and love playing in large bodies of water.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris
  • Average Length: 6-10 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 10-15 years
  • Habitat: Rainforests, swamps, grasslands, and savannas

Tigers can travel many miles to hunt their prey. Eating habits range from large mammals such as wild boar and buffalo to smaller animals including sambar deer and monkeys.
